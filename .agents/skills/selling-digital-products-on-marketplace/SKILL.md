# Selling Digital Products on Marketplace

## When to use this skill

- Generating titles and descriptions for Facebook Marketplace.
- Selling "services" (web design, software, ecommerce) as "packaged digital products".
- Bypassing platform restrictions that disallow service listings.

## Workflow

1.  **Identify Product**: Determine if it's a Landing Page, Ecommerce, Spa Web, etc.
2.  **Productize**: Frame the service as a "ready-made", "closed", or "packaged" product.
3.  **Generate Content**: Use the template to create Title, Price, and Description.
4.  **Obfuscate Contact**: Format the phone number using emojis and text to avoid automated filters.

## Instructions

### 1. Productization Strategy
Facebook Marketplace often flags services. To avoid this, use the following terminology:
- Use "Producto digital listo" (Ready digital product) instead of "Servicio de diseño".
- Use "Versión final NO editable" (Final non-editable version) to imply a finished good.
- Frame the offer as a "pack" or "kit" (e.g., "Landing Page Digital Lista").

### 2. Format Requirements
The output must follow this structure strictly:

**[Title]**
**[Price] · Disponible**
**[Location]**
**Detalles**
- **Estado**: Nuevo
- **Marca**: [Brand/Initials]

**[Main Description]** (Emphasize that it is a finished/packaged product)

**>> Qué incluye**
- [Bullet points]

**>> Qué no incluye**
- [Bullet points]

**>> Importante**
- [Warnings about editability/hosting/etc.]

**>> Ideal para**
- [Target niches]

**>> Contacto**
- [Obfuscated Phone Number]

**[Hashtags]**

### 3. Contact Obfuscation
Format the phone number using a mix of numeric emojis, spaces, and text:
- Example: `📱 9️⃣2️⃣1️⃣ · cuatro seis uno · 3️⃣4️⃣5️⃣`
